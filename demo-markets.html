Markets

Reveille Systems provides IT services and solutions to a broad range of businesses, government agencies, and non-profit organizations. Our management and solution delivery teams bring a diverse knowledgebase and years of experience to each engagement. This level of experience and expertise gives us unique insight into our client's business goals and technology requirements. No matter what type of business you own, let Reveille Systems rise to serve your technology needs! A listing of our most common markets we support is displayed in the navigation panel to the rightbelow.

  • Financial
  • Medical
  • Legal
  • Government
  • Non-Profit

Financial

Our goal is to relieve accounting firms and other financial management organizations of information technology concerns allowing office managers, support staff, accountants and financial managers to focus on client cases and portfolios. We understand the complexity of technology confronting financial firms including network security, data protection, practice management, specialized financial systems and applications, and understanding how these systems integrate with the computer and technology environments. We leverage our breadth of technology expertise and vendor partnerships to develop tailored solutions for practices and firms of any size.

Medical

Reveille Systems strives to relieve medical practices and other healthcare organizations of information technology (IT) challenges allowing practice administrators, office managers, and practitioners to focus on patient health through health information technology. Our experienced management and technology teams bring depth and breadth of technology expertise to medical IT practices, clinics, and other healthcare organizations including HIPPAA compliance, electronic medical record (EMR) implementation, practice management software, and specialized medical applications. We also bring the required knowledge and understanding to ensure these systems integrate with each customer's computer and technology environments. and in many cases, we leverage our vendor partnerships to bring additional technology expertise to solve unique challenges with any healthcare technology, system, process, or business (of any size) through health information technology.

Non-Profit Organizations

Our goal is to relieve non-profit organizations of information technology concerns allowing office managers, support staff, and volunteers to focus on community services and outreach programs. We understand the complexity of technology confronting these organizations including network security, data protection, organizational management, and understanding how these systems integrate with the computer and technology environments. In addition, we understand and appreciate the funding challenges associated with technology investments, and we work with our clients to maximize technology investments and offer special pricing in an effort to give back to the community.

Government

Revielle Systems has assembled a highly qualified and cleared management team with exceptional individual past performance and experience supporting local, state, and Federal government clients. we bring 60+ years of professional experience as Federal Government employees and contractors supporting the Department of Defense (DOD), the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), the Department of Justice (DOJ), and the National Reconnaissance Office (NRO). We have managed and delivered solutions encompassing the full breadth of information technology from enterprise architecture and service desk support. Reveille Systems has also provided support ot local government agencies including network engineering, product sales and support, and business application analysis, services. We are currently registered as a Small, Woman and Minority (SWaM) owned business by the Virginia Department of Minority Business Enterprise (DMBE).

Legal

At Reveille Systems, we deliver information technology support services to legal practices and related firms allowing office managers, support staff, and attorneys to focus on client cases. Our technicians and engineers understand complexities surrounding computer systems, network security, data protection, and specialized legal applications that confront practices; and we bring knowledge and understanding required to integrate these systems with any technology environment. In some scenarios, we work directly with our vendor partners to develop custom information technology solutions to meet specific customer requirements for legal practices and firms of any size.
